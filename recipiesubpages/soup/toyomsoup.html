TOM YUM GOONG SOUP

Ingridients

  • Lemon Leaves 15 g
  • Lemon Grass 30g
  • Galangal 30kg
  • Lemon Juice 500ml
  • Fish Sauce 250 ml
  • Tom Yum Paste 450g
  • Nam Prik Pao (roasted chili paste) 5g
  • Water 3 liter
  • Prawns Head 500g
  • Shrimp 16/20 size 300g
  • Button mushrooms 100g
  • Coconut Milk 20ml
  • Cilantro leaves 5g

Recipe Directions

  1. . In a large pot, bring the lemongrass, lime leaves, galangal or ginger, and water to a boil and then lower the heat to medium. Add the shrimp, chili peppers, and nam prik pao and simmer just until the shrimp is cooked through.

  2. Reduce the heat to medium-low. Add the mushrooms, fish sauce and lime juice. Ladle the soup into bowls, garnish with cilantro, drops of coconut milk and serve.
