Wagyu Beef Burger

Ingridients

  • Wagyu off cut 200gm
  • French fries or wedges 30gm
  • Burger bun 150 gm
  • Onion 60gm
  • Tomato 30gm
  • Lolo bionda 30gm
  • Fresh parsley 5gm
  • Cooking cream 5gm
  • Panko bread crumbs 7gm
  • Egg 20gm
  • Worcestershire 20gm
  • Dijon mustard 5gm
  • Provolone cheese 3gm
  • Balsamic vinegar 10gm
  • Salt 10gm
  • Pepper 3gm

Recipe Directions

  1. In bowl, beat egg and Cooking Cream with fork, Balsamic Vinegar, Worcestershire, fresh Parsley, mix in bread crumbs, onion, mustard, salt, and pepper.

  2. Mix in beef.

  3. Shape into four 1 cm thick patties. (Make-ahead: Cover and refrigerate for up to 6 hours.

  4. Place patties on greased grill over medium-high heat; close lid and cook, turning patties once, for about 10 minutes or until no longer pink inside.

  5. Place in hamburger buns topped with Provolone Cheese.

  6. Serve with baked truffle potato./p>
