Crisp halloumi

Ingridients

  • Halloumi Cheese 200
  • French fries or wedges 30g
  • Saj bread 200g
  • Olive oil 10g
  • Salt 3g
  • Pepper 1g
  • Rocket leaves 5g
  • labnah 30g
  • Olive slice 10g

Recipe Directions

  1. Grilled the halloumi and set on the side.

  2. Apply the labneh on the saj bread, topped with Rocket leaves, tomato sliced, olive slice, saly and pepper.

  3. Add the halloumi to the labnah

  4. Press your roll till havea crisp and gold color

  5. Serve with parmesan fries or smoked wedges
