Steak Sandwich

Ingridients

  • Tenderloin steak 170gm
  • French fries or wedges 20gm
  • Pesto 10gm
  • Eggplant 40gm
  • Zucchini 40gm
  • Baby Marrow 30gm
  • Shaved Carrots 30gm
  • Mushroom 30gm
  • Mixed herbs 2gm
  • Olive oil 30gm
  • Salt 5gm
  • Focaccia bread 150gm
  • Pepper 5gm

Recipe Directions

  1. Place sliced vegetables in a large bowl or on large tray, Drizzle with olive oil and coat veggies using your hands.

  2. Sprinkle with Italian seasoning and grill 2 minutes per side.

  3. Layer the bread with eggplant, zucchini, carrots and mushroom.

  4. Top with grilled steak add some pesto to the top bun and place it on top of the sandwich.

  5. Plate as pictured and serve with French fries or house salad
