title

WILD ROCKET SALAD

Ingridients

  • Rocket leaves 10gm
  • Balsamic vinegar 5Gm
  • Olive oil 5Gm
  • Beet root 15Gm
  • Honey 3Gm
  • Shallot 5Gm
  • Pears 10Gm
  • Chickpeas 5Gm
  • Dijon mustard 2Gm
  • Feta cheese 10Gm
  • Garlic 1gm
  • Salt 0.5gm
  • Pepper 0.5gm

Recipe Directions

  1. Combine the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, honey, Dijon mustard, shallot, garlic, salt, and black pepper together in a bowl

  2. Whisk it vigorously until thoroughly combined.

  3. Boil the chickpeas and beetroot separately, peel the beetroot and cut into quarters, drain the chickpeas and let it cool

  4. Cut the feta cheese into small cubes, except the feta cheese combines all the ingredients with the dressing and finally top the salad with the cheese

  5. Plate as pictured and serve
