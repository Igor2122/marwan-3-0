Chicken with coriander, garlic and lemon

Ingredients:

  • Chicken 500g
  • Potatoes 150g
  • Onion 20g
  • Fresh Coriander 15g
  • Fresh parsley 15g
  • Fresh Coriander 15g
  • Lemon 10g
  • Garlic 5g
  • Salt 4g
  • Olive oil 60g
  • Pepper 3g

Recipe Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).

  2. Place chicken and potatoes in a large baking dish. Season generously with salt and white pepper, coriander powder, coriander leaves, Parsley leaves.

  3. Onion and garlic In a bowl, stir together garlic, olive oil, and lemon juice. Pour over chicken and potatoes.

  4. . Cover dish with foil.

  5. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes. Remove foil, increase heat to 475 degrees F (245 degrees C), and cook until chicken and potatoes are golden, about 30 minutes.
