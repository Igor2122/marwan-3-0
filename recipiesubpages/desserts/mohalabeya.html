Mohalabeya Chocolate Dome

Ingredients:

  • Fresh milk 100 g
  • Sugar 20 g
  • Vanillas extract 2 g
  • Rose water 5 g
  • Corn flour 8 g
  • Cream 16 g
  • Chocolate 54% 30 g

Recipe Directions

  1. 1. Boil Milk, Vanilla, Sugar

  2. 2. Add Cream, Corn Flour Mixture, and Boil until become thick.

  3. 3. Finally add Rose Water.

  4. 4. Make Chocolate Dome in mold and fill the mixture of Mohalabeya inside.
